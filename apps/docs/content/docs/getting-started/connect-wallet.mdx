# Connect Your Wallet

Stellield uses Privy for secure, flexible wallet authentication. This guide covers all available connection methods.

## Supported Wallet Options

### External Wallets (Recommended)
- **MetaMask**: Most popular Ethereum wallet
- **Coinbase Wallet**: User-friendly option with mobile support
- **WalletConnect**: Connect any WalletConnect-compatible wallet
- **Rainbow**: Modern, user-friendly Ethereum wallet

### Embedded Wallet
- **Email-based**: Create a wallet secured by your email
- No browser extension required
- Automatically created during sign-up

## Connecting with an External Wallet

### Step 1: Click Connect Wallet

In the top-right corner of the header, click the **"Connect Wallet"** button.

### Step 2: Choose Your Wallet

A modal will appear with available wallet options. Select your preferred wallet provider.

### Step 3: Approve Connection

Your wallet extension will prompt you to:
1. **Approve the connection** to Stellield
2. **Sign an authentication message** (no gas required)

> **Security Note**: The signature request does NOT give Stellield permission to move your funds. It only proves you own the wallet address.

### Step 4: Verify Network

Ensure your wallet is connected to **Ethereum Sepolia Testnet**:

- **Chain ID**: 11155111
- **RPC URL**: https://rpc.sepolia.org
- **Currency**: SepoliaETH

If you're on the wrong network, you'll see a warning indicator in the header. Click to switch networks automatically.

## Creating an Embedded Wallet

### Step 1: Select Email Option

When connecting, choose the **"Continue with Email"** option.

### Step 2: Enter Your Email

Provide a valid email address where you can receive verification codes.

### Step 3: Verify Email

Check your inbox for a verification code and enter it in the prompt.

### Step 4: Wallet Created

Your embedded wallet is automatically created and secured by Privy. The wallet address will be displayed in your profile.

## Understanding the Wallet Display

Once connected, the header shows:

### Network Indicator
- **Green dot**: Connected to correct network (Sepolia Testnet)
- **Red dot**: Wrong network detected
- Hover for network details

### Risk Score Badge
- Shows current portfolio risk tier
- Click to see risk distribution breakdown
- Updates based on market conditions

### Wallet Button
Click your wallet address to access:
- **Email**: Associated email address (if applicable)
- **Wallet Type**: Embedded or External
- **Connector**: MetaMask, Coinbase, etc.
- **Balance**: Current ETH balance on Sepolia
- **USD Value**: Estimated value (testnet prices)
- **Copy Address**: Copy your wallet address
- **View Activity**: Transaction history
- **Disconnect**: Sign out and disconnect wallet

## Switching Networks

If you connect to the wrong network:

1. Click the network indicator in the header
2. Follow your wallet's prompts to switch networks
3. Approve the network switch
4. Your balance will update automatically

## Managing Your Wallet

### Viewing Balance
Your current balance updates automatically every 30 seconds using:
- **ValidationCloud RPC** for accurate data
- **CoinGecko API** for USD pricing

### Copying Address
1. Click your wallet button
2. Select **"Copy Address"**
3. Address is copied to clipboard

### Disconnecting
1. Click your wallet button
2. Select **"Disconnect"** (red text at bottom)
3. Confirm disconnection

## Security Best Practices

> **Never Share Private Keys**: Stellield will never ask for your private keys or seed phrase.

> **Verify URLs**: Always ensure you're on the official Stellield domain before connecting.

> **Use Hardware Wallets**: For maximum security, use a hardware wallet with MetaMask.

> **Review Transactions**: Always review transaction details in your wallet before approving.

## Troubleshooting

### "Wrong Network" Warning

**Solution**: Your wallet is not on Sepolia Testnet. Click the network indicator to switch.

### Balance Shows "Loading..."

**Solution**: 
- Wait a few seconds for RPC query to complete
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection

### "Wallet Not Detected"

**Solution**:
- Install the wallet extension (e.g., MetaMask)
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser

### Email Not Receiving Code

**Solution**:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 60 seconds before requesting a new code
- Try a different email address
