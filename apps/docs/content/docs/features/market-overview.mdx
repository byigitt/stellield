## Using Market Data

### For Entry Decisions

**Good Times to Enter**:
- TVL growing consistently
- Liquidity score >85
- Yield stability high
- Positive momentum

**Caution Signs**:
- TVL declining >15% in 7 days
- High APY volatility
- Low volume periods
- Multiple protocol issues

### For Portfolio Management

**Rebalance When**:
- Market sentiment shifts significantly
- Your protocols underperform average by >20%
- Risk distribution changes
- New high-quality opportunities emerge

### For Risk Assessment

**Monitor Daily**:
- Your protocols' TVL trends
- Risk tier changes
- Yield vs. average
- Liquidity scores
